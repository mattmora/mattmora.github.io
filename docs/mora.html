Why do I sometimes go by mattmora?

Matt Wang is a such a common name that any reasonable variation of it is usually taken as a username (or domain name!). Mora is my mom's last name, which is of course much less common, so I use that instead.

Besides that, I assume it's better for SEO, the alliteration is nice, and I see using either name where appropriate as an acknowledgment of my identity as a mixed race person.

But I'm fine with being called whatever - Matt, Matthew, Wang, Mora.
